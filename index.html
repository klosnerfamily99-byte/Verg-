Desktop: WASD to move, click the screen once then move the mouse to look, Space or click to fire.
Mobile: left thumb to move, drag anywhere on the right to look around, tap FIRE to shoot where you're looking.
Everyone drops in lined up on the edge of the island's safe zone — you can't move until the 5-second countdown hits zero. Each player spawns already holding one random power-up, revealed to you as the countdown runs.
The island is bigger now — a full match runs about 5 minutes. Forests and rocks ring the outside; a city sits in the middle where you can find SMGs, rifles, shotguns, and sniper rifles lying around (everyone starts with just a pistol).
Stay inside the cyan wall. Last one standing wins.
Casual: 1st +3 wins · 2nd +2 · 3rd +1 · 4th +0 · 5th and below lose wins. Every 10 wins (then every 20 past 50) earns a Power Box — open it for coins or a rare power-up, and spend coins to upgrade any power-up up to 5 times.
Ranked: climb Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Champion. Higher ranks face tougher bots, and rewards shrink each promotion.
